About Us

Saint Mina Retreat Center lies on forty-five acres of land located in Mio, Michigan. Saint Mina Coptic Orthodox Church is located on the property of St. Mina Retreat Center for religious services and quiet meditation. The newly completed building houses sleeping rooms for family and youth retreats, a reception area with a library, meeting rooms, and kitchen amenities. The location of the land provides a wonderful opportunity for those who wish to leave the outside world behind and enjoy the serenity of a remote wooded area surrounding a gorgeous body of water. Such peaceful ambiance truly offers the person an opportunity to examine the soul and find calmness and positive energy. You are invited to participate in a moving and magnificent time meditating on the Word of God from the Holy Scriptures and ways to apply it in your life. St. Mina Retreat Center will provide you with the proper atmosphere to achieve this goal. You are encouraged to take full advantage of what St. Mina Retreat Center has to offer.
